Lasagna

The Italian favorite of lasagne or lasagna that we all know and love originated in Italy in the city of Naples during the Middle Ages.

One of the first references to modern-day lasagne can be found in a 14th-century English cookbook hat highlighted a dish with layers of pasta without the tomatoes.
